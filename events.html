VISITING PROFESSOR OF AERONAUTICS

It is our pleasure to announce that Prof
Sheila Widnall from the Massachusetts

NIGHT IN THE MUSEUM

Get your family together for an
exciting night in the museum as you
sleep over beside dinosaurs and
science displays.

Bring your own sleeping bag and get
ready to rough it as we go exploring
the wonders of science.

ENERGETICA EXHIBITION ON LOAN

On loan from the NEMO Science Museum in
Amsterdam, the Energetica exhibition is coming
to the Science Museum.

It’s a series of installations that allow visitors to
experience the power of the elements as we
harness them.

From solar energy powering lighting, to ‘Wind Island’
that shows how turbines are able to use and control
wind to create power.
