LASER THEATRE

The Laser Show involves the use of laser
light to entertain an audience and makes
it convenient to understand the use of
lasers in the field of
scientific entertainment.

If you ask, our volunteers will inform you
about its various applications in life.

Get transported to a land of magic and
experience sudden shots of fog with a
stupendous mix of sound and laser beams.
